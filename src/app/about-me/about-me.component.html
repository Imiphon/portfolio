About me

I am currently expanding my skill set into web development through a course at DeveloperAkademie, focusing on front-end development. These new skills enable me to further develop my creative and technical abilities in the digital realm, offering exciting opportunities to merge musical and visual art forms.

In 2018 I moved to Lévis, canada with my family and I look forward to applying these skills in an environment that values and fosters innovation and creativity in the field of front-end development. I am available for work in germany and/or Canada.

As a versatile music producer and arranger with years of experience in the music industry, I’ve developed a profound understanding of creativity and technical excellence. My career began with a focus on instrumental performances and music degree for jazz-trumpet, piano and composition in Berlin, but I found my true calling in the realms of studio production and arrangement.

In 1998, I founded the ‘Prokult’ music studio in Berlin-Babelsberg (Germany) followed by ‘Montecruz’ in 2007 in the center of berlin. In these studios, I executed projects ranging from advertising jingles and soundtracks for multimedia and video games to extensive musical arrangements. This work required not only musical expertise but also a deep understanding of the technological aspects of music production and leading music-productions.

A significant milestone in my career came in 2014 when I co-founded a game development company. There, I developed various concepts for musical learning games, wrote a children’s and youth book about the “magic of music,” designed a card game and much more. These experiences enhanced my skills in creative concept development, leading app-productions and designing interactive educational materials.
